Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecommunications company to identify key drivers behind customer attrition. The analysis helps stakeholders understand customer retention challenges, revenue impact, and customer segments most likely to leave the service.
The dashboard provides an interactive view of customer demographics, contract types, internet services, payment methods, tenure patterns, and revenue metrics to support data-driven business decisions.

Key Insights
1. High Overall Churn Rate
Approximately 26.54% of customers have churned.
More than one out of every four customers leaves the company.
2. Month-to-Month Contracts Drive Churn
Customers on month-to-month contracts show the highest churn count.
Long-term contracts significantly improve customer retention.
3. Early Tenure Customers Are More Vulnerable
Churn is highest during the initial months of customer tenure.
Customer retention efforts should focus on onboarding and engagement during the first year.
4. Fiber Optic Customers Show Higher Churn
Fiber optic users represent the largest proportion of churned customers.
This may indicate pricing concerns, service quality issues, or competitive alternatives.
5. Electronic Check Users Churn More Frequently
Customers using electronic checks display the highest churn levels compared to other payment methods.
Automatic payment methods appear associated with better retention.
6. Dependency Status Influences Churn
Customers without dependents show higher churn levels than those with dependents.
7. Gender Has Minimal Impact
Churn distribution between male and female customers remains relatively balanced, indicating gender is not a major churn driver.
Business Recommendations
Improve Customer Onboarding
Develop targeted onboarding programs for new customers during their first 12 months.
Promote Long-Term Contracts
Offer incentives and discounts for one-year and two-year contract plans.
Investigate Fiber Optic Customer Experience
Analyze service quality, pricing, and customer feedback to reduce churn among fiber optic subscribers.
Encourage Automatic Payments
Provide rewards or discounts for customers switching to bank transfers or credit card auto-payments.
Build Retention Campaigns
Target high-risk customer segments with personalized offers and loyalty programs.
